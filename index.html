About Me

The Voice Behind
Your Vision

Victor Muraya is a versatile and professional voice-over artist based in Nairobi, Kenya, with a rich, commanding voice that effortlessly adapts across genres and styles. Whether it's a high-energy commercial, a warm corporate narration, or an intimate podcast introduction, Victor brings authenticity, precision, and presence to every performance.

With a deep understanding of tone, pacing, and emotion, Victor has worked with brands, and content creators across Africa and beyond — delivering audio that doesn't just sound good, it connects.
